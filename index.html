About Us

World Wide Health(W2H) is a website aimed to provide patients access to world-class healthcare by connecting them to thousands of top in their fields practicing physicians. This website makes available to the ordinary people residing in even the most remote places access to excellent doctors from around the world. W2H is created purely for the benefit of the people and hence does not charge any amount for the service.
